{% trans "Who is Jesus?" %}
{% trans "Jesus is the Son of God, sent into the world to save us from our sins. He lived a perfect, sinless life, showed God's love through His teachings and miracles, and willingly gave His life on the cross as a sacrifice for us. Three days later, He rose from the dead, proving His victory over sin and death. Through Jesus, we can know God personally and receive the gift of eternal life." %}
🙏
{% trans "What is Salvation?" %}
{% trans "Salvation means being rescued from the consequences of sin—our separation from God and spiritual death. The Bible teaches that everyone has sinned and falls short of God's perfect standard. Sin breaks our relationship with God and leads to eternal separation from Him." %}
🌟
{% trans "Why Do We Need Salvation?" %}

{% trans "We need salvation because sin separates us from God. On our own, we cannot restore this relationship. But God, in His love, sent Jesus Christ to die for our sins and rise again, so that anyone who believes in Him can be forgiven and reconciled to God." %}

{% trans "Salvation is a free gift, received by faith in Jesus Christ—not by our own efforts or good deeds. Through Him, we receive new life and the promise of eternity with God." %}
